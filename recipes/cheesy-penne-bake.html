Cheesy Penne Bake

Description

This recipe is a nice little twist to regular lasagna. A hearty and filling meal with zucchini, mushrooms, and tomato that is perfect for a date night or the whole family. You can use any type of pasta as well.

Ingredients

  • 4 tsp Olive Oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 Roma Tomato
  • 1 Zucchini
  • 4 oz Baby Bella Mushrooms
  • 6 oz Penne Noodles
  • 5 oz Marinara Sauce
  • 3/4 cups Vegetable Stock
  • 1 1/2 oz Tomato Paste
  • 1 tbsp Italian Seasoning
  • 4 oz Ricotta Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese (shredded)
  • 1 tsp Chili Flakes
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
