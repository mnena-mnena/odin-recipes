Cheesy Penne Bake

Description

This recipe is a nice little twist to regular lasagna. A hearty and filling meal with zucchini, mushrooms, and tomato that is perfect for a date night or the whole family. You can use any type of pasta as well.

Ingredients

  • 5 tsp Olive Oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 Roma Tomato
  • 1 Zucchini
  • 4 oz Baby Bella Mushrooms
  • 6 oz Penne Noodles
  • 5 oz Marinara Sauce
  • 3/4 cups Vegetable Stock
  • 1 1/2 oz Tomato Paste
  • 1 tbsp Italian Seasoning
  • 4 oz Ricotta Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese (shredded)
  • Chili Flakes
  • Parmesan Cheese (grated)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Seasoned Salt and Lemon Pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Farenheit. Wash and dry produce. Dice the tomato and mushrooms. Trim and grate the zucchini using the largest holes of a box grater.
  2. In a medium pan, heat 1 tbsp of olive oil over medium heat. Add mushrooms and season with seasoned salt, lemon pepper, and black pepper. Cook until liquid is released, about 10-15 minutes. Place mushrooms aside and keep the pan ready for step 4.
  3. Bring a medium pot of salted water to a boil. Add noodles and cook, stirring occasionally, until al dente. Drain noodles.
  4. In the medium pan used for the mushrooms, heat a drizzle of oil on medium-high heat. Add tomato and a pinch of salt. Cook for about 3-5 minutes. Add marinara sauce, zucchini, vegetable stock, tomato paste, 1/2 tsp of Italian seasoning, 1 1/2 tsp sugar, 2 tsp olive oil, 3/4 tsp salt, and mushrooms. Bring to a simmer and cook for about 3 more minutes. Remove from heat.
  5. While sauce is simmering, combine ricotta, 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning, and a drizzle of olive oil in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Pour noodles in the pan with the sauce and add 1 tbsp butter. Season with salt and pepper and combine well.
  7. Using an 8x8 pan, spread half the mixture as the bottom layer. Use a spoon to dollop and spead ricotta mixture (OPTIONAL: add some shredded Mozzarella on top of ricotta mixture). Add remaining pasta mixture and top with Mozzarella.
  8. Cover dish with foil that has been coated with non-stick oil. Bake for 10 minutes, remove the foil, then bake for an additional 5-10 minutes. Let it cool. Divide between plates and top with chili flakes and grated parmesan.
