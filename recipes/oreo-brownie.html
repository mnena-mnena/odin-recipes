Oreo Brownies

Description

Brownies and Oreos- the best of both worlds! These brownies are fun to make and even more fun to eat. This recipe is actually a vegan recipe and can be made non-vegan if preferred.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chocolate chips, melted
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 flax eggs, or 2 regular eggs
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 melted coconut oil, or oil of choice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips, unmelted
  • 1 pack of Oreos

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees Farenheit.
  2. To melt chocolate chips, microwave for about a minute and stir to see if chips have melted. If not, continue warming in increments of 30 seconds
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk flour, salt, and baking soda until combined well.
  4. In a seperate bowl, mix eggs, sugar, and oil until combined well, about 2 minutes. Once thoroughly combined, mix water and vanilla extract to the bowl and combine all together.
  5. Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture along with melted chocolate chips. Stir until well combined.
  6. Stir in unmelted chocolate chips. Optionally, take 4-6 crushed Oreos and stir in as well.
  7. Grease an 8x8 baking pan, or line with parchment paper. Pour a little less than half the brownie mixture. Add 9 whole Oreos on top, lining 3 by 3. Pour remaining mixture, covering Oreos. Optionally, add more crushed Oreos on top.
  8. Bake for 35 minutes. Remove from oven and allow to cool completely. Slice into as many pieces desired.
