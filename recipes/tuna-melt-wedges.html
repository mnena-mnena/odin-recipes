Tuna Melt with Potato Wedges

Description

Enjoy this tuna melt sammich with potato wedges as a great lunch idea! Use any canned tuna you'd like and any bread and cheese of your choice as well. This recipe also includes a hommade Parmesan Peppercorn sauce for dipping.

Ingredients

For the Tuna Sandwiches:
  • 1 10 oz can of tuna (drained)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1-2 tbsp sweet relish or dill pickles
  • 1 stalk of celery
  • 1/2 tsp dried dill
  • salt and fresh cracked black pepper to taste
  • 6 slices of butter bread
For the Potato Wedges:
  • 12 oz (about 2-3 medium sized) Russet potatoes
  • 3 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp seasoned salt
  • fresh cracked black pepper to taste
  • Olive oil
For the Parmesan Peppercorn Sauce:
  • 1/4 mayonnaise
  • 1/2 Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp fresh cracked black pepper (plus more to taste)
  • 1 tbsp water
